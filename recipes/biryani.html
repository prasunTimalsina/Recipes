Veg Biryani

Description

A classic dish hailing from the southern part of India, with influences from the Mughals, this vegetarian staple is indulgent, hearty and full of flavour.

Ingredients

  • 200g channa dal
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 medium or 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 3cm piece ginger, finely grated
  • Â½ tsp kashmiri chilli powder or mild chilli powder
  • 4 cloves

Steps

  1. Cook Channa Dal:
    • Simmer channa dal with turmeric for 30 minutes.
    • Reserve some cooking liquid and drain the dal.
  2. Caramelize Onions:
    • Fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Flavor the Dal:
    • Add garlic, ginger, turmeric, chili powder, and yogurt to onions.
    • Mix in cooked channa dal.
  4. Prepare Basmati Rice:
    • Boil rice with spices for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer Biryani:
    • Alternate layers of rice, saffron, mint, coriander, chili, garam masala, rose water, ginger, and channa dal.
    • Top with fried onions.
  6. Finish Layering:
    • Repeat layers.
    • Cover and cook.
